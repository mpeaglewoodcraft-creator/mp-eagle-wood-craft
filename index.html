Our Story

Born From Passion,
Crafted With Soul

MP Eagle Wood Craft was founded in 2018 in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, where the forests whisper ancient craft traditions. What started as a small workshop has grown into a celebrated brand trusted by thousands across India. Every cut, every curve, every finish is done by hand — because we believe machines can never replicate the warmth of human artistry.
